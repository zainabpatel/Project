BLACK VOICES

Black Voices is a collective led by activists Zainab, Hassanat, Iona and Saman, who are enraged about the lack of Black, female representation in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths. They aim to celebrate the achievements of underappreciated, yet crucial women, without whom life as we know it would not be the same. The idea originated from the Black Lives Matter Movement and the need for it to exist beyond the social media bubble.
